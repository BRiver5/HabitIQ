Short description:
Build daily habits, keep streaks, and track progress. Private and fully offline.

Long description:
HabitIQ helps you build better habits and stick with them - one tap a day.

Create your own habits like "Go to the gym", "Read 20 minutes", "Drink water" or
"Meditate", then tap once each day to mark them done. Over time HabitIQ shows your
consistency with a clean year-long contribution grid, streak counters and simple
charts, so you can finally see patterns like "I went to the gym 18 times this month"
or "My longest meditation streak is 48 days".

Designed to be radically simple and calm - no clutter, no feature bloat, no login.

WHY YOU'LL LOVE HABITIQ
- One-tap daily check-off with a satisfying animation and gentle haptic feedback
- Streak counters that keep you motivated to never break the chain
- GitHub-style year grid to visualize your whole year at a glance
- Weekly and monthly bar charts plus clear stats: current streak, longest streak,
  completion rate and total completions
- Custom habits: choose a name, icon, color and frequency (every day, specific
  weekdays, or X times per week)
- Optional daily reminders so you never forget
- Light and dark themes that follow your system
- Drag to reorder, swipe to archive or delete

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
- No account or sign-up required - start instantly
- Works 100% offline; your data stays on your device
- No ads, no tracking, no analytics
- Export all your data to JSON or CSV any time

Whether you want to build a fitness routine, read more, drink more water or simply
stay consistent, HabitIQ gives you a beautiful, distraction-free way to track
smarter and live better.

Track smarter, live better.
